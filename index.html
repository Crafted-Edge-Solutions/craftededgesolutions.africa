Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a custom website?
Typically, a professional business website takes 2-4 weeks. More complex systems like custom booking platforms or dashboards may take 6-8 weeks depending on the requirements.
Do you integrate M-Pesa payments?
Yes! We specialize in M-Pesa STK Push and C2B integrations for websites, booking systems, and automated billing.
Can you automate my manual business processes?
Absolutely. We look at your current workflow (even if it's on paper or Excel) and build custom software or use AI tools to automate repetitive tasks like invoicing, scheduling, and lead management.
Do I need to be tech-savvy to manage the systems?
Not at all. We design our dashboards and systems to be intuitive. Plus, we provide training and ongoing support to ensure you're comfortable using everything we build.
