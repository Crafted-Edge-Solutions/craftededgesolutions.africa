About Us

We build the digital backbone
of growing businesses

Crafted Edge Solutions is a Nairobi-based software and systems integration company. We build practical digital systems — not just websites — that help businesses automate their operations, serve clients better, and grow with confidence.

Our Mission

Practical technology for businesses that are ready to grow

We started Crafted Edge Solutions because we kept seeing the same problem: small and medium businesses in Kenya were being underserved by the technology market. Either the tools were too expensive, too generic, or too complicated for a non-technical business owner to run.

We believe every business — regardless of size — deserves access to the same quality of digital infrastructure that enterprise companies use. Our job is to make that accessible, practical, and affordable.

We're not an agency that disappears after launch. We're a long-term technology partner that helps you grow.

How We Work

From conversation to live system

We keep the process clear and collaborative. No jargon, no scope creep surprises, no disappearing act after launch.

01

Discover

We start with a detailed conversation about your business — your goals, your bottlenecks, and what success looks like. We ask the kind of questions that reveal the actual problem, not just the surface request. No assumptions, no templates pulled out immediately.

02

Design

We map out the full system architecture — what we'll build, how the pieces connect, and what you'll be able to do with it. You see the full picture before a single line of code is written. Wireframes, flow diagrams, and a clear project scope document.

03

Build

We build in focused sprints and share progress as we go. You get access to a live staging environment from week one — so you're reviewing and testing a real working system, not waiting for a final reveal. Feedback is built in, not bolted on.

04

Integrate & Support

We launch, connect all the pieces (payments, automations, notifications, third-party tools), and train your team. After launch, we stay on hand for questions, fixes, and improvements. We don't disappear once the invoice is paid.
