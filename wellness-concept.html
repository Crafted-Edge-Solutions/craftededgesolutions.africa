— Approach

Slow, considered,
specific.

Wellness Solutions is not a chain, not a franchise, and not a "wellness centre" in the way that phrase has been diluted. We're a small practice that treats the person, not the symptom.

Our work happens slowly. First consultations run 90 minutes. Treatment plans take their time. We turn down clients we can't help, and we tell you that honestly.

If you've been everywhere else and felt unseen — this is where the listening starts.

— Offerings

Six disciplines.
One philosophy.

Each modality stands alone or composes with the others — what we recommend depends entirely on you. Sessions are individual, never group, and always with the same practitioner across a treatment arc.

— 01

Functional
Medicine

Root-cause analysis informed by lab work, history, and lived experience. Treatment plans built around the body's actual systems — not symptom suppression. For long-running concerns that haven't responded to conventional care.

— 02

Somatic
Therapy

Body-led work for stress, trauma response, and the kinds of nervous-system dysregulation that talk therapy alone rarely resolves. Gentle, sensory, and grounded in modern polyvagal theory.

— 03

Movement
& Posture

One-on-one corrective movement for pain, alignment, breath, and resilience. Drawn from Pilates, yoga therapy, and physical-therapy fundamentals. Designed for the body you actually live in, not a generic "wellness" template.

— 04

Nutritional
Practice

Food as medicine, treated specifically. We work from your bloodwork and your kitchen, not from internet protocols. Practical, sustainable, and culturally appropriate to East African pantries.

— 05

Manual
Therapy

Therapeutic massage, myofascial release, and structural body work. Quiet rooms, senior practitioners, and 75-minute sessions. Restorative, not transactional.

— 06

Integrative
Plans

For complex cases — chronic illness, recovery, perinatal care — we coordinate across disciplines and stay involved across months. One practitioner leads; the rest support. You always know who's holding the plan.
