What You Get

Everything a business needs
to launch online — fast

No waiting on developers. No expensive agencies. Just a clean workspace where you design your site, publish it, and manage it yourself.

🖱

Visual Drag-and-Drop Editor

Build pages by dragging sections, blocks, and elements into place. What you see is exactly what your visitors see — no preview mode needed.

🏢

Your Own Workspace

Every business gets a fully isolated workspace — your own domain, your own content, your own settings. No shared clutter, no interference from other tenants.

Multi-tenant architecture
💳

M-Pesa & Payments Built In

Add a payment block and your customers can pay via M-Pesa STK Push, card, or subscription — directly from your website. No third-party checkout.

📅

Booking System Included

Drag in a booking block and clients can schedule appointments, pay deposits, and get automated reminders — all without leaving your site.

🤖

AI Content Assistant

Write your first draft with AI. Describe your business and the AI generates headlines, service descriptions, and CTAs in seconds — ready to edit and publish.

Custom Domain & SSL

Connect your own domain in minutes. Every site gets free SSL, fast global CDN, and is optimised for mobile from day one — no technical setup needed.
